# TileManager X – Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: December 1, 2025**

---

## 1. Introduction
TileManager X (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is a puzzle game designed for players of all ages.  
We respect your privacy and are committed to ensuring that no unnecessary data is collected.  
This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

---

## 2. Information We Collect
TileManager X is designed as a fully offline game and **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information**.

### We do NOT collect:
- Name, email, phone number  
- Location data  
- Device identifiers  
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA)  
- Analytics or tracking information  
- Contact list or calendar data  
- Photos, media, or files  
- Gameplay data linked to personal identity  

### Automatically collected data
The App does **not** include analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, or third-party tracking systems.

---

## 3. Local Game Data
The App may store minimal local data needed for core gameplay, including:
- Current game board state  
- Selected grid size (4×4, 5×5, 6×6)  
- Goal target (512, 1024, 2048, 4096, 8192)  
- Current score and best score  

This data:
- Stays **only on the user’s device**  
- Is **not transmitted** externally  
- Is deleted when the App is uninstalled  

No personal information is ever stored.

---

## 4. Use of the Information
Since no personal data is collected:
- No data is used for analytics  
- No data is used for advertising  
- No profiling or tracking occurs  
- No data is shared with third parties  

Local gameplay data is used strictly to preserve game progress and settings.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services
TileManager X does **not** use:
- Third-party analytics  
- Advertising networks  
- Cloud servers  
- Social login systems  
- External APIs  

The App operates fully offline.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy
TileManager X does not collect personal information and is safe for users of all ages.  
Because no identifiable data is collected, parental consent is not required.

---

## 7. Security
Even though no personal data is stored or transmitted, we maintain standard security practices to ensure the App operates safely and reliably.
